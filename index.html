Welcome to IVC Amateur Radio Club N6IVC

We are on a mission to ignite a passion for ham radio among the youth and young adults in our community. With the guidance of experienced operators, we strive to become pioneers of amateur radio, while also eagerly offering our skills to serve the community whenever needed. Join us as we bridge generations through the world of ham radio.

We are located in Irvine, CA. Our club is a student organization ratified by the Inter-Club Council of Irvine Valley College. Yet we strive to extend our small, but growing organization to all the members of our community. There exists an excellent community of amateur radio operators in the United States and the state of California. We can't wait to see you among us. The first step is to join our Discord. There we can assist you better with the studying and licensing process.

All the best,
